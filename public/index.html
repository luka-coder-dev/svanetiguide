Svaneti

Svaneti is known for its beautiful mountains like Zuruldi, Tetnuldi, and others, where you can go hiking or skiing in winter.

Svan towers

Svan towers are common defensive structures in Svaneti, usually 20-25 meters high, with 4-5 floors, and sometimes even 6.

Kubdari

Kubdari is a traditional Svanetian dish made with folded meat dough and secret local ingredients.

You can enjoy trip in Svaneti
no matter the season

spring

Spring in Svaneti is a magical time when the mountains come alive with blooming flowers and melting snow, offering stunning hiking opportunities.

summer

Summer is the perfect season for exploring Svaneti's lush green landscapes, enjoying long daylight hours, and taking part in outdoor activities.

autumn

Autumn brings a riot of colors to Svaneti, with forests turning shades of gold and red, making it a paradise for photographers and nature lovers.

winter

Winter in Svaneti transforms the region into a snowy wonderland, perfect for skiing, snowboarding, and experiencing the unique winter culture.
